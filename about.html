About Us

When we started WebVine, as team members of Orlando Science High School’s competitive math and coding teams, we knew our way around a 3.6 GHz 16 GB processor, and seemingly endless Diophantine equations, but WebVine presented us with a new type of challenge. Creating personalized custom websites, built to accomplish the goals and needs of our clients. What we soon learned, is that it takes a kind of dedication to detail that goes above and beyond skills we honed in the classroom. That’s why, since then, we’ve strived to bring you the best and broadest array of options, as we continue to learn and expand our library of capabilities every day tailoring to the intricate needs of each and every one of our clients.
